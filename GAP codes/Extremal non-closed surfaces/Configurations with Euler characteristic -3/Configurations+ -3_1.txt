There exists exactly 105 subgroups of the Fuchsian triangle group of signature (2,3,22) associated to the extremal 
disc configurations of orientable surfaces that have genus 2, n cusps and b boundary components, where the 
extended number of boundary components n+b is 1.
The corresponding fundamental domains of these subgroups are given by regular 22-agons with all their inner angles 
of measure 2pi/3 and with 1 marked vertices, given by the pairs of consecutive sides of the fundamental domain 
that are identified by an element that fix their common vertex.
